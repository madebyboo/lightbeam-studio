Our Story

A Miami-grown local business focused on quality Japanese matcha and the highest-quality coffee roasts, including premium Colombian beans.

Brand Story

Matcha x Roast was built to bring a calmer kind of café ritual to the city: ingredient-led drinks, precise technique, and a design language that feels warm and elevated.

Our matcha program is rooted in Japanese quality standards, while our coffee program centers around carefully sourced roasts, including standout selections from Colombia.

We believe wellness and luxury can coexist in a simple cup: less noise, better ingredients, and consistent craft every time.
