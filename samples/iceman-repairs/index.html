Common Questions

FAQ

How quickly can you get to my home?

For most service calls in our coverage area, we can dispatch a technician the same day — often within 2-4 hours. Emergency calls are prioritized 24/7.

How much does an AC repair typically cost?

Basic repairs range from $150–$500 depending on the issue. We always provide a written estimate before starting any work. Diagnostic fee is $89 and gets waived if you proceed with the repair.

What brands do you work on?

We service all major brands including Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Rheem, Goodman, Daikin, and more. For installations, we're an authorized dealer for Carrier and Trane.

Do I really need duct cleaning?

In South Florida's humid climate, absolutely. Moisture in your ducts breeds mold, dust mites, and bacteria. We recommend cleaning every 3-5 years, or immediately if you notice musty odors or increased allergies.

Do you offer financing for new AC systems?

Yes! We partner with several financing providers to offer plans with 0% APR for qualified buyers. A new high-efficiency system can actually lower your monthly energy bills enough to offset the payment.

What's included in your maintenance plan?

Our Iceman Club membership ($19/month) includes two full system tune-ups per year, priority scheduling, 15% off all repairs, no overtime charges, and a free drain line flush with every visit.
